What is Lo-Fi?

Lo-Fi (or lofi) is a style of music which usually consists of relaxing and calming instrumental beats. Most lofi songs consist of grainy or textural sounds, giving it a natural and tranquil quality, which contributes to the songs calming vibe

The beauty of lofi is that it is a universal style of music. The lack of vocals in lofi songs and it being purely instrumental beats, with occasional vocal samples, means that it can appeal to any demographic. The low bpm (beats per minute) mixed with the tranquil vibe that lofi provides makes it a perfect medium to listen to when working, studying, relaxing, or any scenario really!
